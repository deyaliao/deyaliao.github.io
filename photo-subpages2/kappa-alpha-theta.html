A sorority that I joined in my junior year and dropped the subsequent year, Kappa Alpha Theta was a prominent memory of my college career. A very public form of community on campus that seemed yet exclusive and mysteriously good or bad depending on whom you talked to, I was fascinated about the role that Greek Life played in many people's lives. While a point of contention within its impacts on my own life, I can confidently attest to its cultivation of true sisterhood for many.
