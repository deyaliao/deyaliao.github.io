A sorority I joined in my junior year and dropped the following, Kappa Alpha Theta is a prominent memory of my college career. A very public organization on campus that seemed exclusive and polarizing depending on whom you talked to, I was fascinated about the role that Greek Life played in people's sense of community. While a point of contention in my own life, I can confidently attest to its cultivation of true sisterhood for many.
